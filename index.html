About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a 19 year old from Sydney, Australia. Currently, I'm a computer science undergraduate student at UTS with aspirations to hone my skills as a software engineer upon graduation. I immerse myself in two particular forms of entertainment: video games and cars. My enthusiasm for the latter led me to join the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) Car Society, a community that shares my love for enjoying and modifying their cars.
